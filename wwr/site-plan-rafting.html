Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to facilitate safe and enjoyable white water rafting experiences at a reasonable price to our consumers.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Our target customers are families and young adults looking for a fun spring break or summer vacation spot. What do they want? They want an easy to plan, easy to execute, fun excersion that won't break the bank. What do they expect to find by accessing the website? They expect to find information about times, dates, and rates. They also likely expect to be able to "reserve" or book a trip and pay for it all online.
