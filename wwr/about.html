History

How we started: Dinosaurs
We started with an idea: How do we capture the same sense of awe and wonder that dinosaurs do? Our founder, Christian Fournier, spent years searching for an attraction that captured this feeling of wonder. It wasn't until he sailed down the Colorado River that he realized what the answer was. White Water Rafting! Now, our goal is to remind you what it was like to be a kid again. We want you to feel that sense of wonder and awe, and as white water rafting professionals, we've tailored our experience to do exactly that!
